# Privacy Policy

Simple YT Tweaks does not collect, transmit, sell, or share user data.

The extension stores feature preferences using Chrome extension storage so your toggles can persist between browser sessions. These preferences stay inside the browser's extension storage system and are not sent to the developer or any third party.

The extension does not use analytics, ads, account systems, tracking pixels, remote code, or external network services.

Simple YT Tweaks is not affiliated with, endorsed by, sponsored by, or otherwise associated with YouTube or Google.
